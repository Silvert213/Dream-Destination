Bali, Indonesia

My dream destination is Bali, an island in Indonesia.

Bali is known for its beautiful beaches, lush verdant forested interior, crystal clear lakes, active volcanoes and wildlife. Bali has vibrant and rich Hindu culture all throughout the island showing off its unique dances, music and art traditions.

Bali is a highly recommened place to visit because of its beautiful beaches, rich culture and history, scenic landscapes, unique temples, delicious cuisine, and various outdoor activities such as surfing and hiking. Bali can be both budget-friendly and expensive to visit, depending on your travel style and preferences. What makes Bali so special to tourist is how you can visit on any budget and have a great time while it also offers many options for luxurious retreats too.
